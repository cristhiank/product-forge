# Vision

PetBoard helps small pet boarding facilities (guarderías) manage bookings, communicate with pet owners, and track revenue — replacing spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups with a simple, purpose-built tool.

## Target Market
Small to medium pet boarding facilities in Latin America (5-50 capacity).

## Core Differentiator
Built for the Latin American market: Spanish-first, WhatsApp-native, simple pricing.
