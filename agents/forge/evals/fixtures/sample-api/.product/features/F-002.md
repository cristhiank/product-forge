# F-002: Notification System

## Job to be Done
When a booking is confirmed or cancelled, the pet owner wants to receive a notification, so they have confirmation without calling back.

## Problem Statement
Currently staff must manually call or text each owner. Often forgotten, leading to no-shows and complaints.

## Open Questions
- Channel: WhatsApp, email, SMS, or all?
- Timing: immediate or batch?
- Who sends: system auto or staff-triggered?
- Template language: Spanish only or bilingual?
